StravaBoards

Data Driven Strava Ratings

Strava, a company that adds a social and gameful aspect to tracking pace based exercises, tracks user workouts via GPS, to determine what well defined paths, or segments, they completed. The metrics that Strava collects allow them to keep an updated leaderboard for users' performance on a segment-wise basis, but currently have no notion of an overall leaderboard. The difficulty of providing this service is certainly proportional to utility it would provide, as objectively comparing users who havenâ€™t completed shared segments is mathematically challenging. My project focuses on providing the foundation for this service by demonstrating that comparing users across a range of segments is possible with the magic of linear algebra!!!
